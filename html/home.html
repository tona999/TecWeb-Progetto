Type 1 Diabetes Calculator

Living with type 1 diabetes can often be challenging. In order to live a healthy life, people with diabetes need to constantly monitor their blood glucose and manage their diet. Diabetes treatment primarily consists of injecting insulin before meals. The amount of insulin to inject mainly depends on the quantity of carbohydrates in the food you eat.

Our calculator makes it easy to determine the insulin dose needed for your meals, be they simple ingredients or complex recipes, and vice versa. On our platform you can save your ingredients, combine them into recipes and work on them at any time. It is free and available for everyone.

Learn how to use our calculator on our instructions page.
